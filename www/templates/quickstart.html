Quickstart Guide

1

Power

Power on your nonbox, give it 10 seconds to boot.

 2

Pair

Tap "nonbox" from the menu. Connect to your nonbox by making sure you enter your nonbox id (located at the bottom of your nonbox), and that you have a green check signifying you are "connected"

 3

Connect

Tap "networks" and choose a Wireless network to connect your nonbox to. Happy surfing :)
